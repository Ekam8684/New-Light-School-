MISSION & VISION

OUR VISION & MISSION


"Silver present for a golden future."

With this as the guiding dictum SIS aims to consider the uniqueness and individual needs of its pupils by delivering a broad, differentiated, and relevant curriculum rooted in culture and values.

Each child is considered a whole person, developing skills, concepts, and attitudes necessary for the opportunities and experiences of the future in order to help prepare them to face real-life challenges.

We endeavour to motivate, support, and inspire personal, spiritual, emotional, and educational growth through a world-class education so that well-rounded, well-adjusted, and socially contributing leaders of tomorrow are produced.

Our mission is to provide a high-quality, comprehensive, and meaningful education for all students. All students will be expected to succeed within the bounds of their abilities and chosen education goals. Each of them will be treated as a unique individual, a lifelong learner, and taught to function as a member of a group and a productive member of society.
