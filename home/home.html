ABOUT OUR SCHOOL


The New Light Public SCHOOL, located at Nai Abadi Abohar, is designated to provide world-class education complemented with the best boarding facilities for boys. It currently offers pre-nursery through senior secondary co-educational facilities that follow the CBSE curriculum. The school campus is artfully landscaped with lush green lawns, flower beds, and children's parks, transforming an almost abandoned patch of land into a tempting place to feast both the eyes and the mind. The school provides amenities such as air-conditioned classrooms, science laboratories, a library, information and communication technology studies, art and craft and performing arts studies, spacious indoor and outdoor play areas, and a splash pool and swimming pool. Being a hi-tech school, now it has stepped into digitization with the thought of "digitised SMGians, digitised Simigos, digitised India."
